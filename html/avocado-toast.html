Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions:

1. Toast the slices of bread to your preference.

2. Mash the avocado in a bowl with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

3. Spread the mashed avocado onto the toast.

4. Sprinkle chili flakes if desired, and drizzle with a little olive oil.

5. Serve immediately.
