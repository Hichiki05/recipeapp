Vegetable Stir-fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (optional)
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over high heat.

2. Add the garlic and ginger, cooking for 30 seconds.

3. Add the bell pepper, carrot, broccoli, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.

4. Stir in the soy sauce and hoisin sauce. Cook for another 2 minutes.

5. Sprinkle with sesame seeds, if using. Serve over cooked rice.
