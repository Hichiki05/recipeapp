Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 150g mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 250°C (480°F).

2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.

3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough.

4. Arrange the mozzarella slices evenly on top.

5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.

6. Top with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil before serving.
