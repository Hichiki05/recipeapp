Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 ball (200g) fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Arrange the tomato slices and mozzarella slices on a serving platter, alternating them.

2. Tuck the basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.

3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

5. Serve immediately or chill briefly before serving.
