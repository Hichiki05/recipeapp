Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving

Instructions:

1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef with the onion and garlic until browned.

2. Drain excess fat and stir in taco seasoning.

3. Add diced tomatoes and simmer for 5 minutes.

4. Warm the tortillas according to package instructions.

5. Fill each tortilla with the beef mixture, lettuce, and cheese.

6. Top with sour cream and salsa, if desired. Serve immediately.
