Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and sliced
  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2. In a skillet, melt the butter and cook the garlic until fragrant.

3. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.

4. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.

5. Add the cooked chicken and pasta, tossing to coat with the sauce.

6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
