Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pancetta
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions:

1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.

2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.

3. Cook pancetta and garlic in a pan until crispy.

4. Combine spaghetti with pancetta and garlic, then mix in the egg and cheese mixture.

5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
