Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, grilled and sliced
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Olive oil and balsamic vinegar for dressing

Instructions:

1. Grill the chicken breasts and slice them thinly.

2. Arrange the salad greens, tomatoes, and cucumbers in a large bowl.

3. Top with grilled chicken slices and crumbled feta.

4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

5. Toss gently and serve immediately.
