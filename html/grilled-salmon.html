Grilled Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill or 1 tsp dried dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

2. In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, dill, salt, and pepper.

3. Brush the salmon fillets with the mixture.

4. Grill the salmon for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through and easily flaked with a fork.

5. Serve with lemon wedges.
